About Us

Welcome to Sad Black Art Tattoo. We are the most underground studio in Odessa. We have been inking traditional tattoos for over 10 years. Ruslan Bauch is a talented craftsman and merchandise who is a highly qualified specialist, who is not limited to one style and produces excellent quality work in all styles. Our studio also employs talented artists who practice different styles and methods of tattooing. We are committed to providing sincere tattoos for our clients.
